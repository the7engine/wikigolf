Wiki Golf

Introduction

Wiki Golf origins date back to when bordem struck the internet.

The intent of the game is to broaden your knowledge whilst efficiently navigating the Internet's encyclopedia.

Find out a little more about the game of Wiki Golf.

Rules of the Game

  1. Only the links in the body of an article maybe used.
  2. You cannot navigate back to a previous page. For this, a penalty stroke will be awarded in addition to the stroke for navigating backwards.
  3. Using Ctrl + F is not in the spirit of the game.
  4. Honesty is the best policy, remember, you're only cheating yourself.

Game Formats

9 Hole Format

A short form of the offical game, good to practice with and kill time on the crapper.


18 Hole Format

Official tournament lengths are 18 holes. Courses are challenging and scores contribute to your handicap.


Pitch 'n' Putt Format

This is the easiest format of the game. Topics are similar and take players on a journey of discovery.


Driving Range

Pick two topics, start at one and get to the other in a few clicks as possible.
